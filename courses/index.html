Areas of Interest

Discover theological studies and learning opportunities.

FOR ADULTS:

Being Church: What has ‘church’ meant in the past, how is this changing, and what can church mean for us today?

Responding to the World: How can we make practical connections between Christian faith and social issues – e.g., climate change, war, poverty, inequality.

Living Worship: What are the essentials of worship, and how can we bring worship to life?

Feasting on the Word: How can we make scripture come alive in relation to real-world realities (Lectionary-based explorations)?

Learning from History: How can Christian history offer insights for the life of faith today?

FOR YOUTH:

Expressing Faith: How can we as youth express our faith through the arts?

Being a Christian Teen in a Secular Society: How can we be faithful amidst peer pressures?

Being in Relationship: How does God want me to love myself, family, friends, & others?

Loving the World: How should youth respond to issues like climate change, social injustice, and war?
