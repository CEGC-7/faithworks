About Faithworks

Faithworks is a joint initiative of parishes with Presbyterian affiliations in Hamilton – though it is ecumenical in its orientation. Its launch is planned for early 2026. Its motto is “CONNECTING FAITH AND LIFE.” It will offer a variety of learning opportunities: short courses, with separate tracks for adults and youth (once-weekly classes for durations of 5-6 weeks); Saturday or weekend workshops or retreats; and a Monday-Friday (daytime) Summer Gathering for all ages.

Faithworks is physically based at St Andrews Presbyterian Church, due to its central location and extensive facilities, but some learning events may be held at other participating churches.

A leadership team consists of volunteer representatives from participating parishes, including a separate youth council. Teachers of courses and facilitators of other learning events are drawn from both clergy and laity with expertise in the particular learning events being offered.

A minimal fee for each course or learning event is suggested, to cover the costs of course materials, publicity, and other operating expenses, but participants can offer a koha based on their own needs.

All learning events are open to the public and ecumenical in nature.

The teaching-learning approach is learner-centred, interactive, and focused on making real-world connections between content and application.
